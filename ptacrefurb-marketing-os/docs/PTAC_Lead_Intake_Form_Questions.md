# PTAC Refurb Lead Intake Form Questions

Use these questions for a Google Form, website form, or manual phone intake.

1. Name
2. Company
3. Role/Title
4. Email
5. Phone
6. Property Type: Hotel, Multifamily, Senior Living, Student Housing, Commercial, Other
7. City and State
8. Approximate number of PTAC units needed
9. Current PTAC brand/model if known
10. Voltage if known
11. Heat type if known
12. Timeline: Immediate, 30 days, 60-90 days, Planning ahead
13. Are you replacing a single unit or planning a larger replacement project?
14. Notes or special requirements

Recommended confirmation message:
Thank you for contacting PTAC Refurb. Our team will review your request and follow up with next steps.
